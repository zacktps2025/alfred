# Alfred Funnels — Conversion Architecture

> Every funnel is a conversation. Make it a good one.

## When to Activate

Use this skill when:
- Designing a new funnel or offer
- Building or reviewing a landing page
- Creating lead magnets
- Optimizing conversion rates
- Planning a launch sequence

## Funnel Types

### 1. Lead Magnet Funnel
**Traffic → Landing Page → Lead Magnet → Email Nurture → Sales**
- Best for: B2B services, consultancies, agencies building authority
- Lead magnet: guide, checklist, template, mini-course, audit
- Conversion benchmark: 20-40% opt-in rate on a good landing page

### 2. Direct Offer Funnel
**Traffic → Sales Page → Checkout → Upsell → Thank You**
- Best for: productized services, courses, software trials
- Key: the offer must be irresistible at the entry price
- Conversion benchmark: 2-5% purchase rate from cold traffic

### 3. Application Funnel
**Traffic → Landing Page → Application Form → Call → Close**
- Best for: high-ticket services ($5K+), agency retainers, consulting
- The application creates commitment and filters unqualified leads
- Conversion benchmark: 10-20% application rate, 30-50% show rate

### 4. Webinar Funnel
**Traffic → Registration → Webinar → Offer → Follow-Up**
- Best for: education-first selling, complex solutions, trust building
- Registration to attendance ratio: aim for 30-40%
- Conversion benchmark: 5-15% of attendees purchase

## Offer Architecture

### The Irresistible Offer Formula
An offer is not a price. It's the complete package of value.

**Components:**
1. **Core deliverable** — What they actually get
2. **Bonuses** — Added value that increases perceived worth
3. **Risk reversal** — Guarantee that removes fear (money-back, performance guarantee)
4. **Urgency** — Real reason to act now (limited spots, price increase, launch window)
5. **Social proof** — Evidence that others succeeded

### Pricing Psychology
- Anchor high, sell at the real price. Show what similar solutions cost.
- Break monthly cost into daily ("Less than $3/day" vs "$90/month")
- Three-tier pricing: Good / Better / Best. Most people pick the middle.
- The price is the last thing they see, not the first. Value before cost.

## Landing Page Structure

### The Conversion Page Blueprint

**Section 1: Hero (Above the Fold)**
- Headline: Specific outcome + timeframe. "Get 30 qualified leads per month without cold calling."
- Subheadline: How it works in one sentence.
- CTA button: Action-oriented. "Start My Free Trial" not "Submit"
- Social proof bar: logos, "Trusted by 200+ agencies", or star rating

**Section 2: Problem Agitation**
- Name the pain they experience daily (3-5 specific pain points)
- Show you understand their world better than they do
- This builds trust. If you can diagnose the problem, they trust you have the cure.

**Section 3: Solution**
- Introduce your approach (not features — the transformation)
- Before/After comparison
- 3 key benefits (outcomes, not features)

**Section 4: How It Works**
- 3-step process (simple, achievable, clear)
- Step 1: Sign up / Apply / Book a call
- Step 2: We set up / audit / create
- Step 3: You get results / see ROI / scale

**Section 5: Social Proof**
- Testimonials with specific results and names
- Case study highlights
- Logos of clients served

**Section 6: Offer Breakdown**
- Everything included (stack the value)
- Bonuses listed with perceived value
- Guarantee explained

**Section 7: FAQ**
- Address the top 5 objections as questions
- "How long does it take?" / "What if it doesn't work?" / "What's included?"

**Section 8: Final CTA**
- Repeat the headline and CTA
- Add urgency if real (not fake countdown timers)

### Landing Page Rules
- One page, one offer, one CTA. Never give them options to leave.
- Remove navigation. No header links, no footer links, no distractions.
- Mobile-first. 70%+ of traffic is mobile. Design for thumbs, not cursors.
- Load time under 3 seconds. Every extra second costs 7% conversion.
- Use real images of real people. Stock photos kill trust.
- Button color doesn't matter as much as button copy. Make the CTA specific.

## Lead Magnet Design

### The 5 Rules of Lead Magnets
1. **Solves one specific problem** — not "The Ultimate Guide to Everything"
2. **Delivers quick value** — consumable in under 10 minutes
3. **Demonstrates expertise** — shows you know what you're talking about
4. **Creates desire for more** — naturally leads to your paid offer
5. **Has a compelling title** — "The 7-Figure Agency Checklist" not "A Guide to Agency Management"

### High-Converting Lead Magnet Types
| Type | Best For | Conversion Rate |
|------|----------|----------------|
| Checklist | Process-oriented audiences | 30-50% |
| Template | "Give me the shortcut" audiences | 25-40% |
| Mini audit | Service-based businesses | 20-35% |
| Calculator | Data-driven audiences | 15-30% |
| Video training | Complex topics | 15-25% |

## Conversion Optimization

### The CRO Audit Checklist
- [ ] Is the headline specific and outcome-focused?
- [ ] Is there only ONE CTA on the page?
- [ ] Is the CTA visible without scrolling?
- [ ] Does the page load in under 3 seconds?
- [ ] Is the form as short as possible? (Name + Email for lead gen)
- [ ] Is there social proof above the fold?
- [ ] Does the page work on mobile?
- [ ] Is the copy scannable? (short paragraphs, headers, bullets)
- [ ] Is there a clear value proposition in the first 5 seconds?
- [ ] Is the offer clear and specific?

### What to Test First
1. Headline (biggest impact)
2. CTA copy and placement
3. Social proof type and position
4. Form length
5. Page layout (long vs. short)
